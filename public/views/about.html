About Us

Project Details

Group members involved with POW-B Analytics are Sonia Pitrola, Nolan Walker, Mark O'Donnell and James Bergeron. For more information about the developers, see the brief bio's shown below. We pride ourselves on details and accuracy so enjoy.

POW-B was created as a partial requirement for the completion of the Mount Royal University Comp4513 Web III, taught by Randy Connolly.
The code we developed can be viewed and cloned with permission from our Github repository. For more project details please go to the Documentation page.

The Developer Leader

Mark O'Donnell

The team (minus 1) got together and decided that we should celebrate the guidance and leadership that Mark has provided the team. Mark has an unassuming style, which belies his knowledge of web based programming tools and techniques. He likely could have completed this assignment in less time than the rest of us took to learn Angular. However, he knew that we wanted to learn the tools and practice implementing the techniques we were learning, so he offered to guide us. He was able to inspire us to overcome obstacles, nudge us when needed and offer insightful hints when the challenges seemed insurmountable. Mark is a natural born teacher. Patient and respectful in his dealings with others. He is driven by coding excellence, yet able to resist unilaterally changing the work of others. He shows professional coding structures, explains them, allows others to adapt them and has the skills and talents to repair the quirkiest of coding attempts. Mark is a highly valued team member. We consider ourselves lucky to have been partnered with a pro. All three of us would highly recommend Mark for his interpersonal, programming, leadership, teaching, time management and listening skills.
